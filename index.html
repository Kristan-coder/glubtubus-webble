Development Timeline of Alienware Computers

Milestones

  • 1996: Alienware is Founded Alienware is founded by Nelson Gonzalez and Alex Aguila in Miami, Florida.
  • 2006: Acquisition by Dell Dell acquires Alienware, allowing the brand to benefit from Dell's resources while maintaining its distinct design and branding.
  • 2010: Introduction of Alienware M11x Alienware introduces the M11x, a compact and powerful gaming laptop that gains attention for its performance in a small form factor.
  • 2016: Launch of Alienware Graphics Amplifier Alienware releases the Graphics Amplifier, an external graphics card enclosure that allows users to boost their laptop's graphical performance by connecting a desktop graphics card.
  • 2020: Alienware Area-51m R2 Alienware unveils the Area-51m R2, a high-end gaming laptop featuring upgradable components, including desktop-grade processors and graphics cards
