    2016: Launch of Alienware Graphics Amplifier

    Alienware's release of the Graphics Amplifier represented a groundbreaking innovation in the gaming world, offering users the ability to enhance their laptop's graphical capabilities significantly. This external graphics card enclosure provided a seamless way to connect a desktop-grade graphics card, unlocking unparalleled gaming performance on Alienware laptops
